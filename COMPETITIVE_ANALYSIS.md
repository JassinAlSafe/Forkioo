### Core Features

#### 1. **Invoicing**
- Unlimited invoice creation
- Branded PDF generation
- Email delivery with tracking (sent, viewed, paid)
- Payment reminders (manual)
- Status tracking (draft, sent, partial, paid, overdue)
- **Missing**: Automated payment reminders, recurring invoices (unclear)

#### 2. **Bank Integration**
- Powered by Plaid or similar (not explicitly stated)
- Automatic transaction import
- "Smart accounting templates" for categorization
- Manual reconciliation required for ambiguous transactions
- **Missing**: Real-time sync status, bulk categorization

#### 3. **Expense Management**
- Receipt capture (photo upload)
- Mobile app for on-the-go expenses
- Approval workflows for team expenses
- Automatic data extraction (amount, date)
- **Missing**: OCR accuracy unclear, no mention of mileage tracking

#### 4. **Bookkeeping**
- Double-entry accounting system
- Chart of accounts (pre-configured templates)
- Manual journal entries
- Bank reconciliation
- **Missing**: Audit trail transparency, version history

#### 5. **Reporting**
- Profit & Loss (P&L) statement
- Balance sheet
- Cash flow report (unclear if available)
- VAT returns (automated)
- **Missing**: Custom reports, benchmarking, predictive insights

#### 6. **Tax Compliance**
- Making Tax Digital (MTD) compliant (UK)
- Automated VAT returns
- Year-end closing support
- **Missing**: Tax optimization recommendations, deadline reminders

#### 7. **Team Collaboration**
- Unlimited users (across all plans)
- Role-based permissions (unclear granularity)
- Accountant access
- **Missing**: Activity feed, commenting, task assignments

#### 8. **AI Features**
- "Smart accounting templates" (rule-based categorization)
- Automatic transaction suggestions
- **Marketing claim vs. Reality**: Branded as "AI-powered" but appears to be mostly rule-based matching, not machine learning

## Strategic Opportunities

### 1. **Trust Recovery Opportunity**

**Context:** Bokio destroyed trust by removing their free plan.

**Our Strategy:**
- Launch with **generous free tier** (no bait-and-switch)
- Transparent pricing on homepage
- Pricing guarantee: "We'll never remove features from your plan"

### 2. **Intelligence Differentiation**

**Context:** All competitors are reactive. You log in, they show data. You figure out what to do.

**Our Strategy:**
- **Smart To-Do List** (Day 1 feature)
  - Prioritizes: VAT due > Overdue invoices > Bank reconciliation > Review unusual expenses
  - Learns your patterns: "You usually send invoices on Fridays. Ready to create this week's?"
  - Estimates time: "2 minutes" next to each task

- **Proactive Insights**
  - "Your AWS spend is 3x normal this month. Want to review?"
  - "Your revenue is up 15% vs. last month ðŸŽ‰"

  - Breakdown: Cash flow (healthy), Profitability (improving), Tax readiness (needs attention)

**Value Proposition:**
> "Your accounting software should tell you what to do, not just what happened."

## Risks & Mitigations

### Risk 1: "Free users cost too much"

**Mitigation:**
- Limit resource-intensive features (reports to 30 days history on free plan)
- Gentle prompts to upgrade when hitting limits
- Free users become advocates (word of mouth = low CAC)

### Risk 2: "Bokio will copy our features"

**Mitigation:**
- They're owned by Visma (slow-moving corporate)
- Our advantage is speed of iteration
- Build community/brand loyalty (harder to copy)

### Risk 3: "We can't compete with QuickBooks' brand"

**Mitigation:**
- We're not competing for enterprise customers
- Target: Bokio's abandoned free-tier users
- Positioning: "The anti-QuickBooks" (simple, modern, fair)
